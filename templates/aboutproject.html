About AI Eval

Welcome to Paper Evaluation Using Artificial Intelligence, an innovative platform developed with the vision of transforming traditional evaluation systems into smart, efficient, and unbiased processes. This project is built by a team of passionate individuals — Arsh Goel, I Benjamen, S Vishwa Avadhan Reddy, and L Varsha — who identified the challenges in manual paper correction such as time consumption, inconsistency, and human bias.

Our system leverages the power of Artificial Intelligence to automate the evaluation of answer sheets with accuracy and consistency. By using advanced techniques in Natural Language Processing and Machine Learning, the platform analyzes responses, assigns marks, and provides meaningful feedback. This not only reduces the workload of educators but also ensures fair and transparent assessment for students.

At its core, our mission is to enhance the education system by integrating intelligent solutions that save time, improve accuracy, and promote scalability. With this platform, we aim to bridge the gap between traditional evaluation methods and modern technological advancements, making assessments smarter and more reliable.
